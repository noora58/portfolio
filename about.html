Hi, I am Nooraah, nice to meet you!

I am a designer and art director currently living in Stuttgart, Germany. Half German, half Italian, I like to bring the best of both worlds into my design which is known for its sophisticated simplicity and thoughtful details.

In 2012 I founded my own creative studio to specialize in branding, web, and print design and to work with ambitious business owners from all over the globe.
